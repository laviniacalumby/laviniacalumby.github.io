Physical Activity Readiness Questionnaire (PAR-Q)

Your Health & Safety First

The PAR-Q is a standard health screening used by fitness professionals worldwide before beginning any exercise program. It helps me understand if there are any health considerations I should be aware of so I can design the safest and most effective program for you.

Please answer all questions honestly. If you are unsure about any question, answer YES as a precaution. If you answer YES to any question, I may ask you to get clearance from your doctor before we begin.
