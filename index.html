About Lavinia

A Holistic Approach to Lasting Change

I know what it feels like to be pulled in every direction as a wife, a mother of two, and a woman with her own ambitions and goals. Life is full, and somewhere in the middle of it all, it's easy to put yourself last.

I created my practice for women who are ready to change that. Whether you're navigating the demands of motherhood, a career, or a life transition, I'm here to support you in stepping into your strongest era. To live with real energy, feel powerful in your body, and show up as your best self for you and everyone around you.

Rooted in both science and lived experience, my approach goes beyond the gym. We work on the habits, nutrition, and mindset that create real, lasting change, meeting you exactly where you are.

With over a decade as an international model, I also bring a deep understanding of the body and what it truly takes to feel and look your best from the inside out. I work exclusively one-on-one, in the privacy of your home, with the discretion and professionalism you deserve.
